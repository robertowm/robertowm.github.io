Today I migrated my personal page from Gatsby to **Eleventy**.

Why?
- Gatsby was too complex for a simple personal page.
- 11ty is much faster and zero-JS by default.
- I wanted total control over the CSS and themes.

I've also implemented a few themes: Synthwave, Doom, Brutalist, Terminal, and Glass. 

Stay tuned for more updates.
